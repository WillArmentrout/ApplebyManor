What Do We Believe?

The Appleby Manor Memorial Presbyterian Church is dedicated to spreading the light and love of Jesus Christ. Together, we worship, study, pray, sing, make decisions, serve the needy, nurture the young, visit the sick and shut in, comfort the grieving, and share the saving message of Christ Jesus, our Lord. Just as we have many different names and faces, we have a variety of gifts with which God has blessed us, and which God calls us to offer in our never-ending mission of sharing the healing power and loving friendship of Jesus Christ to the world. Knowing that we must always grow in the faith, we trust that the Spirit will continue to open our minds and hearts to new paths of service, and continue to increase our willingness to serve faithfully.
